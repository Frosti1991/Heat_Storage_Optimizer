Description of dataset:

time_rounded: timestamp
meter_id: unique meter id for each customer
energy_heat_kwh: heat meter counter in kwh
volume_flow_m3: volume flow in district heating in m3
current_power: diff of energy_heat_kwh between two timesteps - can be interpreted as current power consumption
t_supply - suppy temperature
t_return - return temperature

power_hp: max. power of heat pump
power_solarthermic: max. power of solarthermic
power_waste_heat: max. power of waste_heat
